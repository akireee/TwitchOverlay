A custom, minimal Twitch client

Watch a stream and participate in chat from a small window on the corner of your screen. Easy for multitasking and doing other things while watching Twitch.

Download  View on GitHub 

Watch and chat in your favorite streams

TwitchOverlay's no-BS design shows only what matters. View chat and stream in a simplified way. A configurable background transparency can help you focus more on what's behind the window.

Let your friends know what you're watching

With a Discord Rich Presence (RPC) integration, you'll see a detailed message on your profile about the stream that you're watching on TwitchOverlay.

Minimized view for ease

If you prefer, you can collapse the TwitchOverlay chat and show a slightly larger version of the stream. Because everyone needs a little fun when they work.
